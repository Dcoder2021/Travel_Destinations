Top 3 Landmarks to Visit

Landmark List

The Eiffel Tower in Paris, the Statue of Liberty in New York, and the Great Wall of China are among the most iconic landmarks in the world. The Eiffel Tower, an iron lattice tower, is a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world. The Statue of Liberty, a gift from France to the United States, symbolizes freedom and democracy. The Great Wall of China, an ancient series of walls and fortifications, was built to protect Chinese states and empires against invasions.
